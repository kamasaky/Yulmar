CORPORATE CULTURE

SOCIAL CONDITIONS

Corporate culture is a key factor determining the success and stability of the company «Yulmar Service». It binds employees together, increases staff loyalty to the company and productivity.


    The company has its own residential complex in the watch town:


    Two 2-storey blocks and one 3-storey block, cafeteria, medical room and laundry.


    The total number of staff is 1,550 people.

MEETINGS

Regular meetings with the set of tasks and summing up the results of work contribute to the team's unity on the implementation of the company’s and customer’s policies.


    Improving knowledge in the natural working environment, with mandatory internship under the guidance of a mentor, significantly speeds up the learning process.


    According to TCO and company policies, much attention is paid to the safety training.

TRAINING

Training of the company’s employees is necessary to fulfill the company’s strategic goals and is one of the main priorities in the formation of plans and budgets for training.


    Development of the policy in the field of training of Kazakhstani workers is one of the main goals of the company.


    At the moment, the training is conducted by specialized training facilities in Atyrau and Kulsary, as well as by the ITR company on new methods of performing insulation works.






PARTNERS


LLP "Yulmar-Service" has a successful experience of fulfillment of orders for one of the most famous in the world drilling company "Parker Drilling" at oil field facilities,including directly on drilling rigs.

In April 2010, our company together with the British company “Novus Sealing Limited” established a joint enterprise for the production of gaskets, seals for the oil and gas industry. The project of LLP «Novus Sealing Caspian» entered the industrialization map of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
